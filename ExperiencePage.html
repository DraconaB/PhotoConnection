Just finished this portrait session with Alex in my home studio. Our goal was a relaxed, editorial look that captures a genuine moment of deep thought. To get this shot, I used my Canon camera with a 50mm lens and kept the settings simple to let in lots of light. The lighting was mostly natural from a big window, plus one softbox off to the side to soften the shadows. It was a great, creative collaboration! If you're a model or creative looking to network, send me a message!

For this beauty shoot, we wanted to create something ethereal and focused. We brought together a fantastic team in a bright studio to make it happen. The star of the show was our lighting setup: a central ring light that created that beautiful, signature sparkle in the model's eyes, complemented by large reflectors that bounced soft light everywhere to eliminate harsh shadows.
